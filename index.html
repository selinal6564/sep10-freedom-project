Architecture

Context

  • Architecture is the process of planning, designing and building structures.
  • Architects would make designs and plan how to make their structures functional, safe and make other alterations.
  • Throughout the years, technology has grown and has changed and impacted architecture in many ways.
  • It has helped architects save time and made the process easier for architects. New technologies like apps and gadgets have been helpful.
