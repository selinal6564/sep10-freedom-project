Architecture

Context

  • Architecture is the process of planning, designing and building structures.
  • Architects would make designs and plan how to make their structures functional, safe and make other alterations.
  • Throughout the years, technology has grown and has changed and impacted architecture in many ways.
  • It has helped architects save time and made the process easier for architects. New technologies like apps and gadgets have been helpful.

Technologies in the Present

Software/Apps

Cedreo
  • Available on Web
  • Users can create drawings and complete design proposals
  • Users can draw 2D, 3D and photorealistic floor plans
  • 3D renderings to help visualize design ideas and to create photorealistic renderings (converting 3D models into images)
Sketchup
  • Available on Desktop and Web
  • Acts like a pencil for sketching and designing
  • Users can solve problems during the design process
  • Users can draw 2D floor plans and more
  • Users can give tours of 3D models using AR and VR presentation tools
Morpholio Trace Pro
  • Available on iOS
  • Allows users to draw on top of maps, photos, images, 3D models, background grids, etc.
  • Allows users to give virtual tours of their design
  • Users can turn images to custom stencil templates
RoomScan Pro
  • Available on iOS
  • Users can generate a floor plan by scanning the space from a smartphone
  • Users can also create floor plans by drawing by hand or tapping the smartphone against the wall
  • Users can also scan outdoor areas
Construction Master Pro
  • Available on iOS, Android, Windows
  • Users can solve construction math problems, like area, volume, adding stairs, etc.
  • Dimensional math and conversions

Hardware/Gadgets

Laser Tape Measure
  • An alternative to a traditional tape measure that makes measuring more convenient
  • Can compute lengths, widths and heights
  • It sends laser light to the surface and calculates the time it takes for the reflection to return
Graphic Tablet
  • Portable device used for sketching and drawing
  • Can be connected to a PC or laptop
  • Saves time when designing
Stylus Pen
  • Convenient for sketching
  • Compatible with touch screen devices such as iPad, tablets, phones
Add-On Camera Lens
  • Improves photography when capturing buildings and other objects
  • Improves lighting and focus
  • Includes wide-angle lens and can also take close-ups
  • Portable and easy to use
VR Goggles/Headset
  • Able to experience virtual 3D experiences and animations in real-time
  • Allows architects to convert their 3D models in VR experiences

Possible Technology for the Future

My idea is an app that will make the planning and designing process easier.
  • Architects can actually see their plan on the building site, before construction starts.
  • Architects can input their plan/design in the app, face the camera where the actual structure would go and it would appear there.
  • The app will have a 3D camera, where architects can see their plan/design in its surroundings.
  • It can also give any additional recommendations on how it can fit in better with its surroundings
  • This can help architects visualize their plans better and make any changes that they need to make. Architects can make changes quickly on the app and visualize them.
